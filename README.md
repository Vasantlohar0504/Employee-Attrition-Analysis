An end-to-end **HR Analytics & Machine Learning project** that analyzes employee attrition patterns and predicts employee churn using an interactive data dashboard.

This project demonstrates **data analysis, machine learning, and dashboard development** in a production-style project structure.

The dashboard allows HR teams and business leaders to explore attrition trends, identify risk factors, and predict employee attrition.

# 📊 Business Insights

Key insights discovered during the analysis:

- Employees working **overtime frequently** have higher attrition rates.
- Employees with **lower salaries** are more likely to leave.
- Most attrition occurs within the **first 3 years**.
- The **Sales department** shows relatively higher employee turnover.
- Employees with **low work-life balance** are more likely to leave.
